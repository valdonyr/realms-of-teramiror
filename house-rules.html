Gameplay Rules

  • Critical Hits

    Whenever a player lands a natural 20, they get to choose between rolling the usual dice and re-rolling any 1's, once. Or else rolling just one dice (1's ar not re-rolled) while maxing the other one

  • Cantrips and Spells - the Bonus Action

    Players can cast two spells in one turn as long as one of them has the Bonus Action tag, without the other one needing to be a Cantrip.

  • Death Saving Throws

    Death Saves are rolled by the player but no one can see the result (not even the said player. Eg, using an "upside-down" non-transparent cup)
