Astronomy

The Calendar - Current Year: 2218

A year consist of 250 days of:

  • Frostdeep (Winter) - 88 days (35% of the year)
  • Bloomtide (Spring) - 37 days (15% of the year)
  • Highsun (Summer) - 88 days (35% of the year)
  • Duskhollow (Autumn) – 37 days (15% of the year)

Each "week" (there are 25 weeks in a year) is called a Tenday, like Forgotten Realms: Day the First, Day the Second, and so on.

There are two moons, Aelun the male (50 cycle) and Myrielle the female (20 cycle)

Races and Classes

Elves, Humans, Dwarves, Halflings, Dragonborn, Gnomes, and Orcs are the most common races spread around Alrais. Tieflings, Aasimar, and Goliath exist too, but are less common

The most common classes are Fighters, Rangers, Bards, Barbarians, Clerics, Paladins, Rogues, and Wizards. Druids, Monks, Sorcerers and Warlocks are less common but still found around Alrais.

Religious Orders and other institutions (secret or otherwise)

Most religious orders are for Clerics and Paladins, who follow one or more of the deities' dogmas or philosophies (see the Deity Pantheon, below).

While is it "known" that other Orders exists, they are mostly either secret (like Rogues' guilds), irrelevant or ignored.
